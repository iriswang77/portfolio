React JavaScript Figma UI/UX Design Digital Twin Energy Systems

Project Overview

DisTwi represents a cutting-edge approach to energy management through digital twin technology. This React-based web platform provides real-time visualization and control of energy systems, enabling sustainable energy optimization for the Bauhaus Energy Hub.

As part of the Software Engineering Lab at Bauhaus University Weimar, I led the frontend development and UX design, creating an intuitive interface that transforms complex energy data into actionable insights. The platform demonstrates how thoughtful design can make sophisticated technology accessible to diverse user groups.

UX Design Process

The design process was driven by extensive user research and iterative testing, ensuring the platform meets the diverse needs of energy managers, researchers, and facility operators.

1

User Research & Stakeholder Interviews

Conducted interviews with 12 energy management professionals to understand pain points in current systems. Identified key user personas and their specific workflow requirements.

2

Information Architecture & Wireframing

Developed comprehensive site maps and user flows. Created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on data hierarchy and navigation patterns for complex energy systems.

3

Visual Design & Prototyping

Designed high-fidelity mockups in Figma, establishing a design system that balances aesthetic appeal with functional clarity. Created interactive prototypes for user testing.

4

Iterative Development & Testing

Implemented designs in React with continuous user feedback integration. Conducted usability testing sessions and refined the interface based on real-world usage patterns.

Platform Features

The DisTwi platform integrates advanced digital twin technology with user-friendly interfaces to provide comprehensive energy management capabilities.

🌐

Real-Time Digital Twin

Live synchronization with physical energy systems, providing accurate real-time representation of energy flows, consumption patterns, and system performance.

📊

Interactive Dashboards

Customizable dashboards with drag-and-drop widgets, allowing users to personalize their view of energy data and create role-specific interfaces.

🔮

Predictive Analytics

AI-powered forecasting algorithms that predict energy consumption patterns, optimize resource allocation, and identify potential system issues before they occur.

⚡

Energy Optimization

Intelligent recommendations for energy efficiency improvements, automated system adjustments, and sustainable energy usage optimization.

🔗

System Integration

Seamless integration with existing energy management systems, IoT devices, and third-party platforms through robust API connections.

📱

Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface that adapts to desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, ensuring accessibility for on-the-go energy management.

Development Approach

The platform was built using modern React development practices, emphasizing component reusability, state management efficiency, and performance optimization. The user-centered design approach ensured that complex technical capabilities remain accessible through intuitive interfaces.
