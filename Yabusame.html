Yabusame VR

Immersive Cultural Heritage Experience

An innovative VR serious game that bridges entertainment and education by digitizing Japanese cultural heritage. Developed at University of Tübingen, this project transforms traditional Japanese scroll paintings into an immersive 3D experience, allowing users to experience the ancient art of mounted archery while learning about Japanese history and culture.
